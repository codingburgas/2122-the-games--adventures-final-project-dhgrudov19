This is a game in which you have to adapt to the rural way of life. The main goal of the game is to collect resources with which to build afterwards or to sell them on the market from where you can dig them.

Some information about the game!



As you can see above the time in the game changes. Also in the market you can meet other players with whom you can live, communicate and work together to achieve much greater results. Something else in the game are caves. In them you can dig resources and fight monsters which after killing give you other specific resources.
